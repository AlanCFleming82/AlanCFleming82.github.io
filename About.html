The Workbook of Latin Grammar is researched and written by Alan Fleming, an independent scholar with a Ph.D. in Classical Studies from Indiana University (2016). In addition to working on this project, Alan offers web-based tutoring (via skype) in Latin grammar and in reading Classical Latin.

If you have any comments, feedback, or suggestions about the Workbook (or if you would like to inquire about tutoring), please contact me.

Thanks to Dr. Laura Brant and Dr. Erick Carballo who have provided invaluable feedback and critique in the composition of these lessons.

The website is designed and maintained by Amy Yarnell.
